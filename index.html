I was thinking about an old assignment from university, and I decided that it would be fun to remake it in JavaScript.

This is my attempt at a disease simulator - from a starting group of individuals, we model the spread of a disease through the population, and we can see how the different traits of the disease affect its spread.
  • Mortality: How lethal the disease is.
  • Recoverability: How likely you are to get over the disease.
  • Infectiousness: How effectively the disease passes from person to person.

Use the three sliders at the top to adjust the different disease stats, and the reset button to reset the population.
